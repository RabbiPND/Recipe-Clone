Hot dogs

A hot dog is a food consisting of a grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun. The term hot dog can refer to the sausage itself. The sausage used is a wiener or a frankfurter. The names of these sausages commonly refer to their assembled dish.

Hot dogs recipe

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Hot dog bun(s)
  • Onion
  • chilli pepper
  • Toppings
  • Beef hot dogs

Steps:

  1. Heat up the grill
  2. Place hot dogs on the grill at an angle to create diagonal grill marks
  3. Grill hot dogs for one minute on each side, with the onion(s)/chilli pepper
  4. Transfer hot dogs to buns
  5. Add your favourite topping
